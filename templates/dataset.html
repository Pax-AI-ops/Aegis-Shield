OMEGA-ALIGN Open Dataset

Home About Projects

The OMEGA-ALIGN dataset is a growing multilingual collection focused on antifascism, pro-democracy resilience, ethical AI, and global cybersecurity standards.

Includes:

  • Confucian and Kantian alignment prompts
  • Global compliance rules (e.g., GDPR, AI Act, GB/T)
  • Security scoring (CVSS, NIST, ISO/IEC)

Developers and nations may request access for educational or defensive use. Licensing is Apache 2.0.
